ADMINISTRATIVE OFFICER (FINANCE /ADMISSION)

About Ms.L.Soive


Ms. Lynee Siove serves as the Administrative Officer for Finance and Admissions at Waigani Christian College (WCC) Tertiary Education. With a strong background in administration and student support, she plays a key role in ensuring the smooth operation of financial services, enrolment processes and front-line student engagement within the institution.

Ms. Siove is responsible for coordinating student admissions, maintaining accurate financial records and supporting the college’s operational efficiency. Her professionalism, attention to detail and commitment to excellent service make her an essential part of the WCC administrative team. She works closely with students, parents and academic staff to ensure that all financial and admission-related matters are handled with clarity, transparency and integrity.

Her friendly approach, strong interpersonal skills and dedication to supporting students reflect the values of Waigani Christian College. Ms. Siove contributes significantly to creating an organised, welcoming and student-focused environment within WCC Tertiary Education.
