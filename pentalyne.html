Human Resource Associate

Pentalyne Arasina Pokana is a dedicated and results-oriented Human Resource Associate serving the tertiary education sector at Waigani Christian College. With a strong foundation in psychology and leadership, she brings a holistic approach to HR operations—ensuring that recruitment, onboarding, compliance, and employee relations align with the institution’s Christian values and educational mission. Her commitment to fostering a positive workplace culture and streamlining HR processes makes her a vital contributor to the college’s operational success.

Core Competencies

• Recruitment & Onboarding: End-to-end management of hiring processes, ensuring smooth transitions for new staff.
• HR Policy & Compliance: Upholds institutional standards and national Labor laws within the education sector.
• Employee Relations: First point of contact for staff concerns, conflict resolution, and morale-building initiatives.
• Records & Payroll Support: Maintains secure employee records and supports payroll documentation.
• Performance Administration: Coordinates review cycles and tracks professional development compliance.

Education & Certifications
• Bachelor of Arts in Psychology – University of Papua New Guinea
• Leadership Certificate – Tertiary Student Christian Fellowship (Former President)
• Tour Guide Certificate – Port Moresby Nature Park

Career Journey
Pentalyne’s professional experience spans both corporate and nonprofit sectors, including roles at:

• Nestle PNG
• Digicel PNG
• Air Ways Hotels and Apartments
• Port Moresby Nature Park
Her diverse background has equipped her with exceptional communication, administrative, and people management skills.

Career Ambition
Pentalyne aspires to transition into a strategic HR management role focused on talent development and organizational design within the education sector. She is driven by a passion to uplift tertiary educators and staff while embodying Christian principles in leadership and service.
