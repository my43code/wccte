Who we are

About Papua New Guinea Institute of Higher Education

Papua New Guinea Institute of Higher Education is a newly established christian institute that exists to develop leaders who respond courageously to the social, economic and spiritual needs of Papua New Guinea. Our integrated curriculum balances academic, pastoral care and Melanesian cultural intelligence.

 Core Values

Why Choosing Us

Unlock World-Class Education.

Dreaming of a globally recognized degree without the cost and hassle of studying abroad? Through our partnership with the Philippine Christian University, you can earn an internationally accredited qualification right here in Papua New Guinea.

A Second Chance to Shine.

We believe in potential. If you've faced barriers to higher education, our "second chance" philosophy offers the support and opportunity you need to succeed and reach your academic goals.

Educating the Person: Mind, Body, and Spirit.

Experience a learning environment deeply rooted in strong Christian values. Beyond academics, we focus on holistic development, shaping your character, ethics, and spiritual growth to prepare you for a purposeful life.

Meeting Needs, Building Future.

We're directly addressing the growing demand for qualified professionals in Papua New Guinea. By choosing us, you're not just getting an education; you're becoming part of the solution, gaining relevant skills that empower you to contribute to our nation's progress.

Focusing on Relevant Programs.

We also have a focus on programs like Early Childhood Teacher Education directly aligns with critical national needs and policies, showing responsiveness to the country's development priorities.

Led by Vision, Build for Tomorrow

Benefit from the committed leadership of our founder and a clear vision to grow into PNG’s Christian University. We're investing in modern facilities and expanding our programs, ensuring a future-focused learning experience with qualified staff dedicated to your success
