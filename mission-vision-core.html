Mission · Vision · Core Values

Our compass for Christ-centered tertiary education

We align every decision, classroom and community partnership with the Gospel so that students grow into wise, courageous and service-ready leaders.

  • Faith-first formation for scholars and staff
  • Academic excellence guided by Christian ethics
  • Unity in diversity across every province

“For the Spirit God gave us does not make us timid, but gives us power, love and self-discipline.”

- 2 Timothy 1:7

Mission

We commit to provide transformative tertiary education that equips students to thrive spiritually, academically and professionally.

  • Foster Christian character grounded in scriptural principles.
  • Deliver globally relevant, technologically advanced programs.
  • Promote ethical leadership and lifelong learning.
  • Offer accessible, affordable education to second-chance learners.

Vision

We aim to be the preferred higher education institution, producing Christ-centred transformational leaders and citizens who value unity in diversity and strive for academic excellence.

Vision 2030 focus

Every graduate is equipped to serve communities with courage, creativity and the confidence that comes from faith-filled scholarship.

Partnership mindset

Local parishes, national agencies and international partners collaborate with us to broaden pathways and keep education accessible.

Lifelong formation

Mentoring, research opportunities and community engagement ensure students grow as whole persons long after graduation.

Core Values

Our values keep faith and excellence moving in the same direction.

Patriotism

Love for the country expressed through service, respect and stewardship of national resources.

  • Leadership that honours Papua New Guinea
  • Community projects in every province
  • Responsible citizenship training

Nature

Care for the environment through sustainable campus practices and creation-focused learning.

  • Green campus initiatives and recycling
  • Environmental science partnerships
  • Service days that restore local habitats

Growth

Personal development and excellence that encourage every learner to reach their God-given potential.

  • Coaching for academic and spiritual growth
  • Innovation labs and digital learning
  • Scholarships that reward resilience

Christ

Love for Jesus demonstrated through worship, discipleship and compassionate service.

  • Daily prayer, chaplaincy and retreats
  • Scripture-integrated curriculum
  • Service-learning in parishes

Unity

Love for people and family that keeps our campuses safe, inclusive and welcoming.

  • Peer mentoring and wellbeing support
  • Inclusive student and family events
  • Safeguarding practices for every learner

Guiding commitments

Mission, vision and values translate into daily promises our staff and students can count on.

Christ-centered scholarship

Curriculum, research and community outreach are grounded in gospel values.

Safe & inclusive campuses

Wellbeing frameworks and safeguarding protocols protect staff, students and families.

Whole-person care

Counselling, pastoral care and flexible learning options help every learner stay on track.

Community partnerships

We walk with churches, communities and employers so our graduates keep serving long after graduation.
