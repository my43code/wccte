Addnission and Enrollment Process

Step 1

Application for Admission

1. Obtain the and fill the application form.

2. Prepare supporting documents.

3. Pay application fee to the Bank

4. Submit the application form and supporting documents to the Addmission office, Port Moresby, Papua New Guinea Christain Institude of Higher education.

Step 2

Evaluation and Acceptance.

1. Application Review by the Evaluator.

2. Receive notice of acceptance (letter of offer).

3. Pay tuition and Miscellaneous Fee (if applicable).

Step 3

Registration and Enrollment

1. Proceed to PCU Waigani Campus for Academic Advising.

2. Perform PCU Online Registration (PCU Portal).

3. Ensure access to PCU Portal, LMS, E-Library, Email, etc.

Step 4

Uniform and ID Issance

1. Ensure payment of ID and Uniform fees.

2. Submit ID Requirements.

3. Uniform measurement and collection.

4. Issuance of ID card Uniform.

Step 5

Student Orientation

1. Know orientation schedule date and time.

2. Bring Registration Form.

3. Attend Mandatory Student Orientation.
